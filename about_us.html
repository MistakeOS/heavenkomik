Something About Us

HeavenKomik is a webcomic application and foundation thay aims to give users quality webcomics from various authors and artists, while also providing a source of income for said authors or artists. We give users a bunch of comics to read that can be accessed with ease from a touch (or a click!).

HeavenKomik in 2020

Our Story

2010

HeavenKomik offline store

HeavenKomik offline store provides merchandises, art, and physical comics from various artists for users to buy.

2015

HeavenKomik planning about open an webtoons

HeavenKomik first plan about making a webtoon due to various artists that needs more recognition and a an income for their living. for that matter, heavenkomik created this platform for authors to gain profit from making what they love or what they are passionate about. also, it gives users a place to read many comics from various authors as well, it's a win-win situation!

2019

HeavenKomik and teams start to make a web comic

Our teams gather various information and researches on how to support the artists while providing access to comics for our free users. We started by making two types of comic users, one being free and one being "premium". The only difference between the two is that premium users can read comics unlimited while free users needs to wait or watch ads to read comics.

2020

HeavenKomik launch as an web comic

After we settle on the methods and ways to launch this project into a full-fledged web-comic site that provides users with various comics. We can finally make the site go online with the help of supporters and sponsors.
